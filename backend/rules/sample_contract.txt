RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

1. The Tenant shall pay rent of Rs. 12,000 payable in advance on or before the 5th day of each month.

2. The Tenant shall deposit six months rent as deposit, and the Landlord shall forfeit the deposit if the Tenant vacates before 11 months for any reason whatsoever.

3. The Landlord may evict the Tenant and require them to vacate within 24 hours at the Landlord's sole discretion, without notice.

4. The Landlord may change these terms at any time without notice to the Tenant.

5. If the Tenant is employed by the Landlord, the employer may withhold wages and salary will be forfeited if the Tenant resigns without three months notice.

6. The Tenant shall keep the premises clean and shall not cause damage beyond normal wear and tear.
